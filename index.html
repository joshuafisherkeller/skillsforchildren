Welcome to Skills for Children

Our platform is dedicated to nurturing children's well-being and enhancing their self-efficacy. We offer practical resources for parents, therapists, educators, and professionals to support the development of essential life skills in young minds, especially those who have experienced trauma.

About the Author

Joshua Fisherkeller

Joshua Fisherkeller is a dedicated author and advocate for children's mental health and well-being. Through the Tales of the Resilient Forest series and other therapeutic storybooks, Joshua creates resources that support children, families, and professionals in trauma recovery and emotional development.

Our YouTube channel features entertaining, calming, and educational videos that teach self-regulation and other crucial skills. Our therapeutic books and creative journals provide tools for emotional expression and growth.
