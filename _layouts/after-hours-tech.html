About

Not your typical
tech consultant

I’m Josh Fisherkeller, MSW — a social worker and technologist with a full-time role at the University of Kentucky. After Hours Tech is what I do in my evenings and weekends, because I genuinely enjoy solving technology problems and helping people feel less overwhelmed by the tools they’re supposed to be using.

The MSW isn’t just a credential. It means I explain things without condescension, meet you where you are, and care more about whether something actually works for you than whether it looks impressive on paper.

I’m especially well-suited for therapists and clinicians — people who need tech help but also need someone who understands clinical workflow, documentation requirements, and why confidentiality is non-negotiable.

Because I only work evenings and weekends, I’m available when most consultants aren’t — which is usually exactly when you are.
