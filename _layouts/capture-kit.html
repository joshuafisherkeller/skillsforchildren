Privacy by Design

Nothing leaves the room. Ever.

Every part of this system — recording, storage, and transcription — runs entirely on the local device. There is no cloud account to set up, no subscription to manage, no third-party server that touches your files.

  • No internet required. Sessions record and transcribe whether your router is up or not. Your workflow never depends on your connection.
  • Nothing to breach. Files that never leave the device can’t be exposed in a cloud breach. There is no online storage to compromise.
  • No subscriptions. No ongoing cloud storage fees. No account to renew. The system works the same on day one as it does on year five.
  • You decide where files go. Recordings stay on the local drive until you choose to move or archive them — on your timeline, on your terms.

FAQ

Common questions

Is this HIPAA-compliant? ▼
The system records locally to a dedicated PC and stores files on the local drive. No recordings pass through any cloud server or third-party service. Where data lives and who accesses it remains entirely under your control — the same as any file on your own computer. As always, consult your compliance advisor about your specific obligations.
How difficult is this to set up? ▼
With White Glove service, not difficult at all — for you. We handle the physical mount, cable routing, software configuration, and transcription setup. On the day we leave, you press a green button to start recording and a red button to stop. That’s the entire workflow for your staff.
Does this work in a home office or home-based practice? ▼
Yes — most clients are in exactly this situation. The conference camera sits on a shelf or desktop surface and is unobtrusive in any room. The mini PC fits in a cabinet or under a desk. Surface cable routing requires no drilling into walls and is fully reversible. The whole footprint is minimal and professional.
Do I need a separate observation room? ▼
No. The most common setup is a single room — camera on a shelf or surface, mini PC tucked under the desk or in a cabinet, recording controller on the desk. No second room, no observation window, no additional infrastructure needed.
What if something breaks or stops working? ▼
All equipment comes from major retailers with standard manufacturer warranties and easy return policies. 30-day remote support is included with White Glove service. Because the mini PC is dedicated to recording only, most issues are straightforward to diagnose and resolve remotely.
