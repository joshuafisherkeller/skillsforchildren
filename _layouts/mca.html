The Story

In a neighborhood alive with laughter, a curious boy named Maximilian wonders about the cosmos — and dreads the night he'll have to sleep alone.

One evening, his parents reveal a wondrous secret: his bedroom is a doorway to the universe. Tucked into his very own bed for the first time, Maximilian closes his eyes and begins to drift…

From racing rovers on Mars to chasing diamond snowflakes on Uranus, every night becomes a new adventure. But can Maximilian conquer his biggest challenge yet — sleeping in his own room?

A tender tale of courage, discovery, and the power of imagination — for ages 3–8 and the grown-ups who tuck them in.

The Author

Joshua Fisherkeller

Joshua holds a master's degree in Social Work and enjoys helping children and families and working with systems of care in Kentucky. He is also the author of Journey of Brave Friends, a TF-CBT storybook companion.

Beyond his books, he has created a resource platform, skillsforchildren.com, where readers can find all his books, videos, and curated resources.
