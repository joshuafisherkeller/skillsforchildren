Trauma affects brain development, particularly the amygdala (fear response), hippocampus (memory), and prefrontal cortex (decision-making).

The good news: With evidence-based support like TF-CBT (Trauma-Focused Cognitive Behavioral Therapy), co-regulation with trusted adults, and skills like grounding techniques, children's brains can heal and build resilience.
