What is Childhood Trauma?

Childhood trauma refers to experiences that threaten a child's physical or emotional safety, including abuse, neglect, household dysfunction, or exposure to violence. These experiences can have lasting effects on development, but with proper support and evidence-based treatment, healing is possible.

The resources below provide evidence-based information and support for understanding and healing from childhood trauma.
