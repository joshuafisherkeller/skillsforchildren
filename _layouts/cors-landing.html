FAQ

Common questions

Is this HIPAA-compliant? ▼
The system records locally to a dedicated PC and syncs to your existing cloud storage (Google Drive, Dropbox, or OneDrive). No recordings pass through any third-party server. Where data lives and who accesses it remains entirely under your control — the same as any file on your own computer. As always, consult your compliance advisor about your specific obligations.
How difficult is this to set up? ▼
White Glove tier: you press one button on day one, we handle everything else. Remote Setup tier: the mini PC arrives fully configured — you mount the camera, plug things in, and we set up the software together over a 90-minute video call. Most people with no IT background finish comfortably. The self-install guide is written for the same audience.
Does this work in a home office or home-based practice? ▼
Yes — most clients are in exactly this situation. The camera is the size of a smoke detector. The mini PC fits in a cabinet. Surface cable routing requires no drilling into walls and is fully reversible. The whole footprint is minimal and unobtrusive.
Do I need a separate observation room? ▼
No. The most common setup is a single room — camera in the ceiling corner, mini PC tucked under the desk or in a cabinet, Stream Deck on the desk surface. No second room, no observation window, no additional infrastructure needed.
What if something breaks or stops working? ▼
All equipment comes from major retailers with standard manufacturer warranties and easy return policies. Remote troubleshooting is available with any paid service tier. Because the mini PC is dedicated to recording only, most issues are straightforward to diagnose and resolve remotely.
